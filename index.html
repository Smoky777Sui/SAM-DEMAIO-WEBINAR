Army Veteran · CEO · Investor · Podcast Host

Sam DeMaio’s story doesn’t start in a boardroom — it starts at age 19, buying his first duplex and renovating it himself with zero experience. After serving in the United States Army and spending time in law enforcement, Sam launched Showcase Remodels out of a van, surviving on $7-an-hour side jobs to fund his dream.

Today, Showcase Remodels is a 100+ employee powerhouse completing 15–17 projects daily across South Jersey and Philadelphia, with a 4.9-star rating and 400+ five-star reviews. His clients include NFL stars Vinny Curry and Fletcher Cox. He hosts The Relentless Podcast on YouTube with 145+ episodes.
